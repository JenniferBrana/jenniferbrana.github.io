Curriculum Vitae

I am an incoming PhD student in the Computer Science Department at Carnegie Mellon University, beginning June 2023. I will be supported by a NSF Graduate Research Fellowship.

My research interests lie in computer architecture and sustainable computing, specifically in the design of heterogeneous architectures and memory systems.

I did my undergrad in computer science and computer engineering at the University of Portland. As an undergrad, I worked with Nathan Beckmann in CORGi at Carnegie Mellon University. I researched design methodologies for novel cache coherence protocols. Specifically, I focused on designing protocols for cache-attached accelerators.
